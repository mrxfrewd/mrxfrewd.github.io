Canvas Boutique Hotel

Canvas is 49-room boutique hotel located at the heart of Puerto Princesa (a two-minute drive away from the airport). This hotel has modern interiors highlighting the colorful and rich culture of Palawan through curated furniture pieces, which are locally made; hand-painted murals by talented Palawan artists; and art pieces representing the islands. This property is truly a perfect place for the modern traveler.
